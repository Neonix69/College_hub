About College Hub

College Hub is a platform exclusively for Karunya students. It helps seniors share academic resources and helps juniors access them in a structured way.

What you can do here
Browse materials using a clean path: Course -> Course Year -> Semester -> Subject -> Exam Type. Upload internal exam papers, semester papers, notes, and other useful files for your juniors.
Goal
Build a student-driven academic repository where every batch contributes and every next batch benefits.
